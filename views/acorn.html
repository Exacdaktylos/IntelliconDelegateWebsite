Acorn Group

Exclusive Bronze Partner

The Acorn Group, is a diverse conglomerate based in Sri Lanka founded in March 2020 by a team of professionals. The company operates in several sectors, including aviation, inbound/ outbound travel and leisure, and also has diversified into freight & logistics, insurance brokering, and education management. Acorn Group has a regional footprint, operating in Sri Lanka, Maldives, Thailand, Cambodia, UAE and Bangladesh.



AIESEC in SLIIT is a local chapter of AIESEC Sri Lanka, a non-governmental not-for-profit organization. AIESEC is a global youth-led organization striving to achieve peace and fulfillment of humankind's potential by activating leadership qualities in youth through learning from practical experiences in challenging environments. Together with partner organizations, AIESEC facilitates a network of cross-cultural exchanges 9n the form of volunteering experiences and professional internships.
